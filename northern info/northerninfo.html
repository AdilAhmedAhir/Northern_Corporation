About us and What we do:

  • Northern Corporation Ltd. was established in 1987

Established in 1987, Northern Corporation Ltd. has spent decades mastering the art of garment manufacturing. What began as a dedicated facility with high quantity apparel has evolved into a specialized facility that produces both premium high quality and quantity knitwear.

For us, manufacturing is more than just assembling fabric—it is about engineering comfort, durability, and style into every single piece. By combining our deep-rooted heritage of traditional craftsmanship with modern, forward-thinking production techniques, we bridge the gap between classic reliability and modern performance.

Our Core Pillars:

  • Decades of Expertise: With over 35 years in the textile industry, our refined manufacturing processes ensure consistency and excellence at every scale.
  • Uncompromising Quality: From sourcing premium, breathable yarns to rigorous quality control, we are committed to delivering garments that stand the test of time.
  • Future-Focused Innovation: We constantly adapt to modern trends, utilizing state-of-the-art technology to produce high-performance athleisure and everyday comfort wear.

At Northern Corporation Ltd., we don't just manufacture garments; we build the foundation for active, comfortable living.

History

Northern Group – A Brief History

Northern’s journey began in 1967 with the establishment of Northern Steel Re-rolling Mill Limited, founded by Shafiuddin Ahmed and Tosrifa Khatoon.

In 1987, Northern Corporation Limited (NCL) was formed, marking the group’s entry into the textile and ready-made garments (RMG) industry.

The group continued to grow:

  • 2000 – Fashion Asia Limited (FAL) joined the group.
  • 2002 – Tosrifa Industries Limited (TIL) was established as a green-focused textile company.
  • 2005 – Printers and Printers Limited (PPL) was introduced to enhance production efficiency.
  • 2011 – TIL and NCL became public limited companies under RJSC. Enviro Pac Limited was also introduced to strengthen vertical integration.
  • 2015 – TIL was listed on the Dhaka and Chittagong Stock Exchanges.
  • 2016 – Northern achieved LEED certification for its environmentally focused production facilities. NCL also began a major expansion project.
  • 2017 – FAL started an expansion project to increase production capacity.
  • 2018 – NTG Farms joined the group to diversify operations.

Today, Northern is recognized as a leading textile manufacturer in Bangladesh, known for quality, innovation, and sustainability. The group is committed to environmentally friendly production and actively supports the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Northern has been recognized as a BGMEA Best Environment Compliant Factory, is a signatory of the UN Global Compact, and partners with organizations like SNV Netherlands, demonstrating its commitment to responsible and sustainable business practices
